Tic-Tac-Toe

Rules:

1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

2. Players take turns to place their marker (X or O) in an empty cell.

3. The first player to get three of their markers in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.

4. If all cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a tie.
